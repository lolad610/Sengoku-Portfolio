FAQ

Questions

Everything you need to know

What types of projects do you specialize in? +

I specialize in complete game environments, detailed structures, custom maps, and polished UI systems. From fantasy realms to futuristic cities, horror atmospheres to vibrant simulator worlds.

What's your typical turnaround time? +

Simple builds take 3-5 days. Complex environments range from 5-14 days depending on scope. Rush delivery available for premium projects.

What file formats will I receive? +

You'll receive production-ready RBXM/RBXL files that can be directly imported into Roblox Studio. Everything is organized and optimized.

Do you include revisions? +

Yes! Robux packages include 1 revision round, PayPal packages include 2 rounds. Additional revisions available at fair rates.

Can you match any art style? +

Absolutely! Whether realistic, cartoonish, low-poly, detailed, dark, or colorful - I adapt my style to match your game's aesthetic.

Are builds optimized for performance? +

Yes! Every build uses efficient techniques and performance best practices. Beautiful visuals that run smoothly on all devices.
