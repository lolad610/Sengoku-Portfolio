Questions

Everything you need to know

What types of projects do you specialize in? +
I specialize in complete game environments, detailed structures, custom maps, and polished UI systems. From fantasy realms to futuristic cities, horror atmospheres to vibrant simulator worlds - I adapt to match your vision perfectly.
What's your typical turnaround time? +
Simple builds take 3-5 days. Complex environments or complete maps range from 5-14 days depending on scope. I maintain high quality while working efficiently. Rush delivery available for premium projects.
What file formats will I receive? +
You'll receive production-ready RBXM/RBXL files that can be directly imported into Roblox Studio. Everything is organized, optimized, and ready to use immediately.
Do you include revisions? +
Yes! Robux packages include 1 revision round, PayPal packages include 2 rounds. Additional revisions available at fair rates. Your complete satisfaction is my priority.
Can you match any art style or theme? +
Absolutely! Whether your game is realistic, cartoonish, low-poly, detailed, dark, colorful, or anything in between - I adapt my building style to perfectly match your game's aesthetic and target audience.
Are your builds optimized for performance? +
Yes! Every build is optimized using efficient techniques, proper part counts, and performance best practices. Beautiful visuals that run smoothly on all devices.
What if I need changes after delivery? +
You'll see progress updates throughout development. Before final payment, we ensure everything meets your expectations. Post-delivery support and minor adjustments are included.
Do you offer discounts for large projects? +
Yes! For large-scale projects like complete game worlds or multiple maps, I offer attractive package deals and long-term partnership rates. Contact me to discuss your project.
